Best Curve

Game Description

Best Curve is the best game you´ll ever be allowed to play and our claim to quality doesn´t come from nowhere, because we have good lawyers. The aim of this game is to collect points in a race for the highscore. You control a red ball which is constantly moving forward, but you can turn left or right with either the A or D Keys. While moving, you create a line behind you that will, over the course of the game, take over your screen. But you can limit it´s spread by collecting "debuffs". There are two Debuffs in total and whenever you pick one up, the other dissappears! Whenever you collect a debuff, your line shortens and provides you more freedom to move. Furthermore, depending on the amount of the line that got removed, your score rises according to the shortened amount. However, be wary of these nasty debuffs, since they can be quite annoying. Such as the yellow ball, which inverts the controls, the orange ball, which increases your speed, the white ball, which makes you invisible or the pink ball, which increases your curve radius. To combat this you can press the SPACE key on a cooldown to create a gap in your line. You can't have the same debuff active twice at the same time. Furthermore, after 10 seconds the debuffs will despawn and respawn. Strive for a high score before your own creation overtakes you, representing human civilization metaphorically. If you want to return to the start screen you can click the "Best Curve" Icon in the top center of the header.
